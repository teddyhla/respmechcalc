Step 1 : Theoretical Normal Values


Estimated Theoretical FRC is in litres.

Estimated Theoretical Respiratory System Compliance is in ml/cmH20.

Step 2 : Assessment of Respiratory Mechanics

Perform a 5 second inspiratory hold to measure Peak airway Pressure (Ppeak) and Plateau Pressure (Pplat).


Now, perform a 5 secondary expiratory hold to measure total PEEP pressure (PEEPtot).


The driving pressure (deltaP) is in cmH20.

Actual respiratory system compliance (Crs-actual) is in ml/cmH20.

Estimate of an actual FRC is in litres.

Calculated PEEP volume is in mililitres.

Now, press calculate below for further results on airway resistances and mechanical power.

Calculated airway resistances (Raw) is in cmH20/litres/second.

Calculated dynamic strain (Strain dyn) is in dynes.

Calculated total strain (Strain tot) is in dynes.

Calculated mecahnical power of the system (MPrs) is in Joules/min.
